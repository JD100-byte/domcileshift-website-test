Together, the Wiese Law Group efficiently and professionally assists individuals and legal entities seeking to relocate to a new jurisdiction — handling every step of the process: from identifying the optimal tax structure and jurisdiction, to obtaining residency, setting up banking, and ensuring full legal compliance. Whether you are a high-net-worth individual seeking a lower personal tax burden, or a business restructuring its holding company, our team provides clear, actionable advice tailored to your specific circumstances.

Our Founder

Jo Daniel (J.D.) Wiese is a lawyer offering legal assistance in the areas of tax, corporate law, labour law and bankruptcy. Wiese Law Firm is a partner with Nordnet, Scandinavia's largest securities broker-dealer, where the firm manages a comprehensive website with a focus on tax and securities.

Attorney Jo Daniel Wiese assists individuals and businesses with private law issues, including advice and dispute resolution. He previously headed the tax services at Euronext Securities on the Oslo Stock Exchange, and has experience from the Norwegian Tax Administration's Finance Group where he worked on control cases related to financial transactions — including the exemption method, incentive schemes in employment relationships, and additional tax. His legal career began in West Virginia as a Petroleum Landman, handling acquisitions of petroleum rights.

In addition to his legal practice, he is a writer in Auditing and Accounting, and teaches legal subjects at the University of Southeast Norway (USN). Jo Daniel is also the founder of the fintech portal skattebasen.no — the only portal of its kind, with a database of several thousand stocks and funds offering direct purchase of completed tax assessments for securities. It is currently used by investors, accountants and auditors across Norway.
